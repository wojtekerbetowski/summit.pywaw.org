How to participate

Anyone willing to attend PyWaw Summit conference, has to fill the form available at http://summit.pywaw.org/registration and to pay the conference admission fee for his/her participation (if one is applicable). The number of tickets is limited, the registration and ticket purchase shall be closed, once the tickets are sold out / once the number of available tickets is exhausted. An e-mail confirming the participation with payment details shall be sent after filling the registration form. After placing the payment attende shall receive another e-mail, which is a confirmation of a successful registration. The conference participants hereby express their consent to the use of their image (for example in conference pictures) in conference materials (mainly at the internet site).
